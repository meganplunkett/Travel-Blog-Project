Megan Plunkett

Megan Plunkett, one of the creators, wanted to share her experiences in Germany, so that others may enjoy a more personalized travel experiences. She has specifically shared her advice about the city of Berlin, the western city of Münster, and the large southern city of Stuttgart.

Mia Szymkowski

Perhaps the most known of the German cities, Berlin shines brightly as the capital of Germany. The city hosts the greatest amount of citizens and also Angela Merkel- the Chancellor of Germany herself. As Americans living in this city, our experiences had not only consisted of fascinating tours, centering around the Nazi Regime and the Berlin Wall, but a ton of cultural opportunities and excursions.
