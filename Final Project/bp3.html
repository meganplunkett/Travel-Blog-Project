HAMBURG

by Mia and Megan

Overview

Hamburg, a beautiful city known for its prominent harbor, the Port of Hamburg, lies in the Northern part of Germany. It is a major tourist destination, as well as a hub for European science, research, and education. It is also home to two famous concert halls, which helped further the careers of artists such as The Beatles. It is the second-largest city in Germany, and the 8th largest within the European Union.

Points of Interest

As Hamburg is one of the major cities in Germany, there are many interesting and exciting activities to do and sights to see. I only spent two days in Hamburg, and basically just wandered around to get a feel for the city instead of stressing trying to plan out each hour to catch all of the main sightseeing aspects. My favorite part of Hamburg was venturing into the tunnel below.
