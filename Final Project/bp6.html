Personal Favorites

I am slightly biased, but I would say that Hannover is my favorite city in Germany. Hannover is the first city in Germany that I ever went to, and so it means a lot to me. I think it is so beautiful and has so many interesting areas. One of my personal favorite spots is the Maschsee. It is an artificial lake south of the city centre of Hannover. It is the largest body of water within the capital of Lower Saxony. It is a popular area to stroll around, or to participate in water sports in summer. You can also rent a boat and paddle around in the summer.
