STUTTGART

by Mia and Megan

Overview

Stuttgart is a gem of the South, and is also the capital of its german state Baden-Württemberg. The city of about 600,000 holds German tradition in its architecture, food, and annual Christmas Markets that line the streets in winter Time. It is perhaps best known for the Porsche and Mercedes-Benz headquarters, as well as their according museums. In pop culture, famous twins and influencers Lisa & Lena also reside in Stuttgart, though they often are seen traveling.

"Das neue Herz Europas"

This slogan was unveiled in 2008 to represent the city of Stuttgart. As Berlin is often regarded as the cultural zenith of the North, Stuttgart made the slogan to show foreigners and alike that their city too is a hub of diversity. Many, who are unfamiliar with Stuttgart or southern cities, regard them as "snobby" and uneventful. However, both those from Stuttgart- and from my own personal experience- say that southern cities are eventful and surprisingly diverse. In fact, Stuttgart is consistent of 40% immigrants. -Megan
